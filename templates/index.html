Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time for a registered rent agreement?

Typically, you will receive your digital registered rent agreement within 3-4 working days after the biometric verification is completed.

What documents are required for rent agreement registration?

You need Aadhar card and PAN card of the owner, Aadhar card of the tenant, Aadhar cards of two witnesses, and property address proof (electricity bill or Index II).

Is doorstep service available in all areas of Pune?

Yes, we provide doorstep biometric verification services across all areas of Pune and surrounding regions.

What are the government charges for rent agreement registration?

Government charges include stamp duty and registration fees, which vary based on your rent amount, deposit, and tenure. Use our fee calculator to get an accurate estimate.

Can I get a hard copy of the agreement?

Yes, we can print and courier a hard copy to your address for an additional fee. The digital copy is legally valid and widely accepted.

Do both parties need to be present at the same location?

For biometric verification, both owner and tenant need to be present along with two witnesses. If parties are at different locations, we can arrange an additional visit.
