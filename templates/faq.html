A registered rent agreement (Leave & License) is a legally binding document registered with the government that outlines the terms of tenancy between a landlord and tenant. Unlike a notarized agreement, a registered agreement has full legal validity and is widely accepted as address proof.

A notarized agreement is simply verified by a notary but NOT registered with the government. A registered agreement is filed with the Sub-Registrar's office and has full legal enforceability in court. Government bodies like RTO, Passport, and Aadhaar require registered agreements for address proof.

Once you submit documents, our team drafts the agreement within 2 hours. After you approve it, we schedule the biometric visit at your convenience. The actual biometric process takes 15–20 minutes. You receive the registered digital agreement within 3–4 working days from biometric completion.

Required documents:

  • • Owner: Aadhaar Card + PAN Card
  • • Tenant: Aadhaar Card
  • • 2 Witnesses: Aadhaar Card each
  • • Property: Index II or Electricity Bill

All documents can be submitted digitally via WhatsApp or email.

A Leave & License Agreement is the legally correct term for a rent agreement in Pune/Maharashtra under the Maharashtra Rent Control Act. Unlike a Lease Agreement, it grants the licensee (tenant) permission to use the property without creating any tenancy rights. It's more landlord-friendly and is the standard form used for residential and commercial properties.
